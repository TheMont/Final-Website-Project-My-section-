Inspiration

Greetings!

For many people, art, thoughts, and dreams captivate our minds. We want to do something cool, or simply do something to pass time. There are many ways of gaining inspiration from objects, places, or events.

"Explore what you can, and love what you find."

"There are places awaiting your company, you never know what may happen to you!"

Ideas and Tips

There are many strategies to help calm down that busy mind of yours.

Explore New Places!

Consider going to new places nearby. There's no need to go out of the city! Go to a big park, take note of the plants, the wild animals, and scenary you notice. This is especially good for the artists in search of a good scene to draw.

Look for a New Nobby!

Always wanted to try something new? Take the risk! Go and learn to ride a bike, draw something new, or even try out a new kind of food. Try out a new film series. Read a new book whether old or new.

Join a club!

There are endless opportunities to nuture your passion, or a new thing you want to try out! Search out in your area, or go far to search for groups, clubs, or communities. There are SEVERAL sujects that take place in multiple comitees, the most popular focusing on art, dance, coding, some community service here and there, etc.

"Your limit is the world. No one can stop you ,but you!"

"Go on and explore!!"
